# The Pitched Fort — Brand & Logo Guide

A refreshed, boutique brand direction: polished, whimsical, premium — *luxury sleepovers,
unforgettable memories.* This guide covers the logo concept, fonts, colors, and where everything
should appear.

---

## 1. Logo Concept

The refreshed mark keeps your core idea — a cozy **tent / sleepover** — but makes it cleaner, more
intentional, and brand-worthy. The concept is an **elegant line-art teepee** crowned by a **soft
crescent moon and twinkling stars**, with a little **pennant flag** and a blush doorway for warmth.
Clean line work + a serif/script type pairing gives it that elevated, feminine, celebration-ready
feel.

Three live, ready-to-use versions are included:

| File | Variant | Use for |
|---|---|---|
| `logo-primary.svg` | **Primary** — emblem above stacked wordmark + script tagline | Website header area, hero, print, signage |
| `logo-horizontal.svg` | **Alternate** — emblem beside the wordmark | Navigation bar, email signatures, wide spaces |
| `logo-submark.svg` | **Submark / icon** — emblem only | Social profile pictures, favicons, watermarks, stickers |

These are **SVG** files — infinitely scalable, crisp at any size, and easy for a printer or designer
to recolor. They're also rendered live in both versions of the website (see the header and footer).

### Primary logo direction
- Centered emblem (teepee + moon + stars + pennant)
- **"The Pitched Fort"** in an elegant serif beneath it
- **"luxury sleepovers"** in a soft script as a tagline accent

### Alternate / submark direction
- **Alternate:** emblem to the left, "The Pitched Fort" to the right — perfect when vertical space is
  tight (website nav, letterhead, email footer).
- **Submark:** just the emblem in a rounded navy "badge" — ideal as an Instagram/TikTok profile
  picture or a favicon. It reads clearly even at tiny sizes.

## 2. Recommended Fonts

A three-font system — elegant serif, soft script, clean sans — that's whimsical without being
childish. All are **free Google Fonts** (already loaded on the site).

| Role | Font | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Headings (serif)** | **Fraunces** | Elegant with a soft, slightly playful character. Use for titles & the wordmark. |
| **Script accent** | **Sacramento** | The pretty handwritten touch — taglines, "eyebrow" labels, little flourishes. Use sparingly. |
| **Body / supporting (sans)** | **Nunito** | Friendly, rounded, highly readable. Use for paragraphs, buttons, forms, navigation. |

> Tip: keep the script font for short accents only (1–4 words). It's the sprinkle, not the cake.

## 3. Color Palette

Pulled from your DIY Pick-Up & Drop-Off form. Navy gives structure & trust; sage, blush, and gold
add the whimsy; cream keeps it soft and breathable.

| Swatch | Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| ⬛ | **Deep Navy** | `#22395B` | Primary — headings, buttons, footer, structure, trust |
| ⬛ | Navy Deep | `#1A2C46` | Hover states, deepest sections |
| 🟩 | **Sage Green** | `#9DB29A` | Secondary accent — DIY highlights, checks, botanicals |
| 🟩 | Sage Deep | `#7E9579` | Sage text on light backgrounds |
| 🌸 | **Blush Pink** | `#EBB7BE` | Playful accent — CTAs, script text, hearts/bows |
| 🌸 | Blush Deep | `#D98C97` | Blush text, hover states |
| 🟨 | **Warm Gold** | `#D9B26A` | Sparkle — stars, moon, tiny highlights |
| ⬜ | **Soft Cream** | `#FBF5EB` | Background — the calm canvas everything sits on |
| ⬜ | Cream Deep | `#F3E9D8` | Alternating section backgrounds |

**Pairing rules of thumb**
- Navy text on cream = your default.
- Cream text on navy = your "night sky" sections (hero, add-ons, contact, footer).
- Blush = the main call-to-action color (warm + inviting).
- Sage = consistently tied to the **DIY** option, so it reads as a visual cue.
- Gold = only for tiny celestial sparkle. A little goes a long way.

## 4. Where the Logo Should Appear

| Placement | Which version | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Website header (top-left) | **Horizontal** | Already in place — links back to home. |
| Website footer | **Primary** (cream tone) | Already in place on the navy footer. |
| Hero / final CTA | **Submark** | Small emblem as a finishing flourish. |
| Favicon / browser tab | **Submark** | Already wired up. |
| Instagram / TikTok / Facebook profile picture | **Submark** | Crisp in a circle crop. |
| Social post templates, story highlights | **Primary** or **Submark** | Keep generous cream/navy space around it. |
| Flyers, signage, thank-you cards | **Primary** | Pair with one hero photo + lots of breathing room. |
| Email signature | **Horizontal** | Small, beside your contact details. |
| Watermark on photos | **Submark** | Low opacity in a corner. |

**Clear space:** always leave padding around the logo equal to at least the height of the moon —
don't crowd it. **Smallest size:** keep the wordmark legible (~120px wide min for horizontal); below
that, switch to the submark.

## 5. Brand Voice (quick reference)

Warm, polished, parent-friendly, lightly playful, stylish, trustworthy, boutique, celebration-focused.

**Lean into:** dreamy, styled, elevated, unforgettable, cozy, photo-worthy, celebration-ready,
boutique, playful, polished.
**Avoid:** too childish, too corporate, too generic.
